YT-Tutor

            
An AI-assisted tool for studying YouTube videos


            
Getting Started

            
    
                
  1. Paste a YouTube URL into the input bar and press Enter (or click Load).
    2. 
                
  2. The transcript and chapters will load automatically in the right panel.
    3. 
                
  3. Click Copy initialization message to copy video context, then paste it into a new Claude chat.
    4. 
                
  4. Use the Discussion section to copy your position or take notes as you watch.
    5. 
            


            
How to Use

            
    
                
  • Initialize Chat — Generates a context message with transcript, metadata, and chapters to start an AI conversation about the video. Configure what to include with the checkboxes.
    • 
                
  • Choose Your Workflow — Select split screen (AI in another window) or single screen (mobile/small screen) to tailor the discussion tools.
    • 
                
  • Discussion — Two modes: Timestamp shows your live position for copying into your AI chat. Notebook lets you jot notes and questions tagged with timestamps. Switch between them using the tabs.
    • 
            


            
Tips

            
    
                
  • Press T to toggle theatre mode for a wider video view.
    • 
                
  • Click any transcript line to jump to that point in the video.
    • 
                
  • Drag the dividers to resize panels to your preference.
